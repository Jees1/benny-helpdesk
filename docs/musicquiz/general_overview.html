What is Music Quiz?

Music Quiz is a fun module for your users to interact and have fun with one another. It can be fast paced and challenging but that is what makes it fun.

Note: You can earn up to 45 points in total by guessing the Song Title and Author
Note: A lot of messages are sent when playing the game so it is advised to have a different channel for this plugin!
